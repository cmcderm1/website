STORY

My professional background has predominately highlighted my interest in marketing and communications. I received a bachelor's degree in business administration at Ashland University and attained a master's degree in media communication and journalism at Point Park University in Pittsburgh.

During my career, I have working as a freelance web developer, customizing WordPress websites and drafting content for my clients. Additionally, I helped to bring life to Conscious Collaborative, a marketing agency in Pittsburgh that focuses on working with ethical organizations. The beauty of today's modern age is the option that we have to be more methodical about the work that we choose to be a part of. My current position, as the Managing Director at Conscious Collaborative has seriosuly inspired my love for working alongside changemakers and community leaders.

There's nothing more inspiring than working with organizations that are making a real difference in the world, and this is exactly what I seek to do in my future projects as a fullstack web developer.
